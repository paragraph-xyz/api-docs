# **Profile**

Tell readers who you are. These details appear on your author profile, on your posts, and whenever someone hovers over your profile pic across the Paragraph product.

**Where to find it:** Avatar → [**Settings** → **Author**](https://paragraph.com/settings/account/author).

**Fields:**

- **Name** – shown on your posts (real name or pen name).
- **Bio** – up to **500 characters**; a short description that appears on your profile.
- **Profile photo** – upload a square image (recommended **400 × 400 px**).
