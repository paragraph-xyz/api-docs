# **Recommendations**

Recommendations are a powerful way to grow together with other writers on Paragraph. By recommending other publications, you help readers discover new writers you enjoy and create positive-sum growth across Paragraph.

---

## **How It Works**

Right after someone subscribes to your publication, they’ll be shown the list of publications you recommend with the option to subscribe.

![Recommendations Subscribe Flow Pn](/images/recommendations-subscribe-flow.png)

---

## **Adding Recommendations**

1. Go to your **Publication Settings → Growth** tab.
2. Under **Recommendations**, click **Add recommendation**.
3. Enter the publication URL you want to recommend.
4. (Optional) Add a short note about why you recommend it—this can increase trust and clickthroughs.
5. Click **Add** to save.

Your recommendations will now appear to readers after they subscribe to your publication.

---

## **Best Practices**

- **Recommend thoughtfully**: Choose publications your audience will genuinely appreciate.
- **Keep it relevant**: If your writing is about tech, recommend other strong tech writers—this increases conversion.
- **Reciprocate**: Build growth loops by coordinating with other writers to recommend each other.

---

## **Why Use Recommendations?**

- Grow your audience through trusted, word-of-mouth referrals.
- Support and promote other writers you believe in.
- Earn crypto rewards automatically when your referrals lead to meaningful reader actions.
